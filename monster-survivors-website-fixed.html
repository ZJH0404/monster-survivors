About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exhilarating survival game where you must navigate through hordes of relentless monsters. As time progresses, the difficulty increases, testing your skills and strategic thinking.

Inspired by popular roguelite games, Monster Survivors challenges you to survive as long as possible while collecting power-ups and defeating enemies to earn experience points and level up your character.

Game Features

  • Endless waves of diverse monsters
  • Character progression and upgrades
  • Various weapon types and abilities
  • Randomly generated maps
  • Increasing difficulty over time
  • High score tracking

How To Play

Controls

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. The character automatically attacks nearby enemies when possible.

Leveling Up

Defeat monsters to gain experience. When you level up, choose from a selection of upgrades to enhance your abilities.

Surviving

Keep moving to avoid being swarmed by monsters. Collect health items to restore your HP and power-ups to increase your strength.

Tips & Strategies

For Beginners

  • Focus on movement speed upgrades early on
  • Don't stand still - keep moving to avoid enemy swarms
  • Prioritize defensive upgrades for longer survival
  • Learn enemy patterns and behaviors

Advanced Tactics

  • Balance offensive and defensive upgrades
  • Create chokepoints to funnel enemies
  • Save power-ups for when you're surrounded
  • Aim for synergistic ability combinations
